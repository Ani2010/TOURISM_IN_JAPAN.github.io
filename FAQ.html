Frequently Asked Questions

  • How many days should I spend in Japan?

    +

    8-15 days minimum

  • Which is the best time of the year to visit Japan?

    +

    Spring (March to May) is the best time (also the peak time) to visit most of Japan for pleasant weather and cherry blossom, typically in Tokyo and Kyoto.

    Generally speaking, the best time to visit Japan is spring, followed by autumn (September to November). With comfortable weather, you could appreciate stunning natural beauty: the iconic cherry blossom in spring and colourful leaves in autumn.

    If you're willing to risk possible rainy days and hot weather (around 31°C/86°F), summer (June to August) is a perfect time to have a taste of exotic Japanese festivals and enjoy plenty of firework displays.

    However, the rainy season usually lasts from June to mid-July, when you can find the lowest prices and smaller crowds.

    Winter (December to February) is ideal for seeing snow monkeys, enjoying a hot spring dip, and going skiing.
